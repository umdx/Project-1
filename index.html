About Us

History

About CYA Little League Since our inception as Battlefield Little League in 1971, CYA Little League has been a community- based athletic organization dedicated to developing superior citizens. CYA Little League provides a safe, inclusive, and positive environment that focuses on developing the core values of teamwork, discipline, respect, and citizenship in all our youth athletes through the game of baseball.

Values

Develop and continually improve upon positive character traits, including courage, loyalty, and selflessness, which will serve them throughout their lives, not just on the field of play.

Programs

Continue to refine and develop baseball skills and knowledge through their active participation in our baseball program. We are committed to maintaining a positive culture in which coaches, parents, fans, umpires, and players work together to achieve our mission.
